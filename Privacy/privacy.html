    Welcome to our company. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal
    information when you use our products and services.

    • Personal Information: This may include names, contact information, billing details, and any other information you provide to us.
    • Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with our services, including log data, device information, and analytics.
    • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect data on your usage patterns.
  3. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information for :

    • Providing and improving our products and services.
    • Communicating with you, including updates, newsletters, and customer support.

    We may share your information with:

    We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
